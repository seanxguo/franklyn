WECHAT MOBILE MARKETING GAME

During my time as a product management intern in JD.com’s ebook department, I came across a marketing opportunity, and led the ideation and development of a WeChat-based social media marketing game. There were no dedicated official resources, and involved:

  • Obtaining and analyzing over 80 WeChat marketing case studies,
  • Presenting to and convincing my department to provide financial support,
  • Getting buy-in from the development team to create APIs for the game,
  • Negotiating with multiple HTML game companies to minimize costs,
  • Negotiating with other JD.com departments to provide distribution channels to over 500,000 users,
  • Designing game mechanics and user flow, wireframing (check out wireframes here), and
  • Leading a cross-functional team of devs and designers

HOW IT WORKS

The game is publicized on the JD Finance WeChat Official Account as an article and link to the game. The game is set in a classroom; users are 'students' trying to secretly read a novel without the teacher finding out. If the user wins, he/she gets a coupon for a free ebook, which is accessible upon downloading the JDRead app.

RESULTS

We launched the game on September 9, 2016 within four weeks of starting the project. Data is still being collected; the game's efficacy will be evaluated based on UV/PV, k-factor, and app downloads. You can find a non-WeChat compatible prototype of the game here (try it on mobile, though!).
