BRAND IDENTITY

YALE GAP YEAR SOCIETY

As a founding member of the Yale Gap Year Network, I was in charge of developing a visual identity and presence for the group. We wanted the Gap Year Network to look like a long-established and official student organization, but also indicative of the often fun and quirky nature of gap years. The logo was designed in Yale Blue (#0E4C92), with a slab serif font (Museo) reminiscent of college hoodies. A series of posters were designed to be placed on campus during prospective student week ("Bulldog Days") in order to encourage people to consider taking gap years. These provocative posters hinted at various cool adventures that could happen in a gap year, and also sought to challenge people to resist inertia and embrace uncertainty.
