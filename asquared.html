A SQUARED

KICKSTARTER VIDEO

I was approached by A Squared to produce a video for a Kickstarter campaign. A Squared is an a cappella group that uses Ableton Live to remix voices in live performances. I worked with A Squared members and led a small team of videographers to create this project. The campaign successfully raised 114% of its funding goal.
